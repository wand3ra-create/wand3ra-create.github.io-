The Origin Story

Built by a frustrated beginner.

Unda was born out of a simple frustration: I wanted to learn robotics, but the internet makes it unnecessarily hard. The best resources either assume you're already an engineering genius, or they lock the good stuff behind massive paywalls. I got absolutely sick of the gatekeeping and the overcomplicated advice online.

So, I decided to build the platform I wished I had. Unda (Swahili for "to create") is designed for people who don't have access to fancy equipment or mentors. It’s straight to the point, hands-on, and free.
