Our Story

Built from the ground up — literally.

Unda was born out of a simple frustration: the best robotics resources assume you already have access — to equipment, to mentors, to expensive courses. Most people don't.

We believe talent is universal. Opportunity isn't. Unda exists to close that gap — starting with the fundamentals, taught clearly, for free, for anyone.

The name means to create in Swahili. That's the whole idea.

🌍

Built for the underserved

Designed with learners who don't have access to robotics labs or expensive gear — just a device and curiosity.
